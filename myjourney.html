About Me

Hi, I’m Niki, originally from the UK, I’ve travelled to many of the world’s fascinating countries and lived on four different continents. Each time, learning, adapting, communicating, clarifying, sharing, organizing, surviving and managing.

I’ve always loved learning about people and helping them connect and I'm excited to see how humans will continue to evolve together and connect with technology into the future.

My background in psychology helps me understand how and why people think in certain ways.

I have experience working as a UX researcher, which helps me make design decisions backed by data and I understand the importance and necessity of clear and strategically-placed copy to help people use products successfully.

My mission now is to help businesses connect with users and users connect with digital products through asking thoughtful questions and the written word.
